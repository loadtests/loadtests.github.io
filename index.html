Simulate realistic interactions

Write load tests scripts in Lua to simulate a sequence of user actions to test. For example, a script can simulate a user browsing your website, adding products to a shopping cart, and checking out.

On-demand test execution

Execute any of your test scripts on any of your sites as much or as little as you want. Multiple tests can be queued for each site.

Visualize results in real-time

View the status of your load tests in real-time as they execute. Detailed result summaries containing critical information such as response time are available once your test fully completes.
